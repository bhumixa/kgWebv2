About Us

At atisundar, we are driven by beauty - in everything we do, in everything we are and in everything we create. With enormous passion, dedication and attention to even the minutest details, we envision, design, create and bring to you sarees, salwar kameez, kurti and dresses that make you look your angelic self. 

We believe that with atisundar you would feel "very beautiful, very me!" 

atisundar is a trademark of Kamal Rayon Private Limited.

Kamal Rayon Private Limited is a group company of Kamal Textiles and has been incorporated recently to sell sarees and women apparel online.

Kamal Textiles, as a group, had its very humble beginning in 1967 - starting with a small agency in Kanpur. Today, the group has presence in Kanpur, Delhi, Gorakhpur, Bhilwara, Bhiwandi, Mumbai, Ahmedabad and Surat. 

With atisundar.com, we bring our expertise of over 4 decades, directly to you.
